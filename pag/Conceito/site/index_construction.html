Not just stories
but culture

Real people and stories

Myths tell how things came to be the way they are. They tell how deities, men, animals and plants differentiated. The rituals, in turn, they follow the opposite path of the myths.

"The indigenous peoples of Brazil are the original inhabitants of Brazilian territory and were present here before the arrival of Europeans at the end of the 15th century. There is a great diversity of indigenous peoples in Brazil, and the indigenous population, according to criteria from the 2022 Census , is approximately 1.6 million"
"Currently, there are still a series of obstacles in the lives of indigenous peoples in Brazil, such as the delay in demarcating indigenous lands and the disrespect for these demarcations by miners and loggers, who illegally invade these lands."
"If it is impossible to talk about a single indigenous culture, it is impossible to talk about a single religion. Each Brazilian indigenous people has its own belief system, with its rituals, its gods and its legends.

Some of the main characteristics of the religions of indigenous peoples is the figure of the shaman – the shaman (pai’é), in Tupi-Guarani."
